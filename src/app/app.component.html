Requirement for booking of flight

To fly domestically, you will need any recognised form of identification like a driver's license, international passport, voter's card, NYSC ID card, work ID card, etc. Please note that your means of identification will have to be valid for the dates you intend to fly

  
  

Flight Booking FAQs

What are the advantages of online flight booking? 

Through online air ticket booking you can easily compare prices of multiple airlines to get your air tickets at lowest rates. Also, it is easy to do online flight booking as multiple payment options are available on websites like FlyNet.

When should I book to get best flight ticket prices? 

For best flight ticket prices and flight ticket offers, it is recommended to book at least 3 to 4 weeks in advance for domestic air tickets. For international flight ticket it is recommended to book at least 7 to 8 weeks in advance, so that you can get the best flight ticket prices.

How can I book flight tickets online? 

With the help of FlyNet, you can easily book both domestic flight tickets and international air tickets in simple steps within a few seconds.

Why should I make a flight booking from FlyNet? 

Along with an easy flight booking process, FlyNet offers various discounts, instant EMI options and credit/ debit card related offers on flight booking. By availing such benefits, you can book air tickets at reasonable prices.

In how much time, my flight booking will get confirmed? 

After completing the air ticket booking process on FlyNet, you will get an instant confirmation mail and a message on the registered number and email id respectively.
